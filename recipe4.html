🍗 Butter Chicken

Home

Creamy, spicy and full of authentic Indian flavors.

⏱ 60 mins
🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Spices (garam masala, chili powder)

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook chicken until tender.
  3. Prepare butter tomato gravy.
  4. Add cream and mix well.
  5. Combine chicken and simmer.
