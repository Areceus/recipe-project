Sweet Gulab Jamun

Soft, juicy and soaked in sugar syrup.

⏱ 45 mins
🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Ghee (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough using milk powder and flour.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom.
  5. Soak fried balls in syrup.
