Cheesy Delight Pizza

Loaded with cheese and fresh toppings.

⏱ 40 mins
🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onions & capsicum
  • Olives
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Spread sauce on pizza base.
  3. Add toppings and cheese.
  4. Bake for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve hot.
