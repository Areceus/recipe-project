Chocolate Dream Cake

Soft, fluffy and full of chocolate goodness.

⏱ 50 mins
🔥 Medium

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and butter, mix well.
  4. Pour into baking tin.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
