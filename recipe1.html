Creamy Italian Pasta

A delicious classic pasta with rich flavors.

⏱ 30 mins
🔥 Easy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat olive oil and sauté garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and cook.
  4. Mix pasta with sauce.
  5. Top with cheese and serve hot.
